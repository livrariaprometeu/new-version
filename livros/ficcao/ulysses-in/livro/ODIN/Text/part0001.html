Título original: Ulysses

Autor: James Joyce



Ulysses is a modernist novel that follows a single day in the lives of Leopold Bloom, Stephen Dedalus, and Molly Bloom in Dublin on June 16, 1904. Drawing inspiration from Homer's Odyssey, James Joyce transforms an ordinary day into an extraordinary literary journey through memory, identity, desire, language, and the complexities of modern life. Celebrated for its innovative narrative techniques and stream-of-consciousness style, the novel is widely regarded as one of the greatest achievements of twentieth-century literature.
