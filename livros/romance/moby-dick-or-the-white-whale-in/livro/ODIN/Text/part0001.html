Título original: Moby-Dick; or, The Whale

Autor: Herman Melville



Moby-Dick; or, The Whale is a literary classic that follows the sailor Ishmael aboard the whaling ship Pequod under the command of Captain Ahab. Obsessed with finding and killing Moby Dick, the great white whale that cost him his leg, Ahab leads his crew on a perilous voyage across the oceans. Blending adventure, philosophy, and profound reflections on fate, obsession, and humanity's struggle against nature, the novel has become one of the most influential works in American literature.
