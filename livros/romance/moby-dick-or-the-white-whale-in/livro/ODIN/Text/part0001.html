Título original: More Than Skin Deep

Autor: Erle Stanley Gardner






More Than Skin Deep is a contemporary romance that explores how physical attraction often hides deeper emotional needs. The story follows two characters who initially connect on a superficial level but gradually discover each other beyond appearances. As their relationship develops, they confront personal insecurities, past emotional wounds, and the challenge of being truly seen for who they are rather than how they look.
