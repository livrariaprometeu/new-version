Título original: Romeo and Juliet

Autor: William Shakespeare



Romeo and Juliet is a tragic play that tells the story of two young lovers from rival families in the city of Verona. Despite the hatred between the Montagues and the Capulets, Romeo and Juliet fall deeply in love and secretly marry. A series of misunderstandings, conflicts, and unfortunate events ultimately leads to one of the most famous tragedies in world literature, making the play a timeless reflection on love, fate, and the destructive power of hatred.
