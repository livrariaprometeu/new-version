Título original: Alice's Adventures in Wonderland

Autor: Lewis Carroll



Published in 1865, Alice's Adventures in Wonderland is a landmark of children's literature and literary nonsense. The story follows a young girl, Alice, who falls through a rabbit hole into a surreal world filled with strange creatures, logic-defying situations, and absurd encounters.

Throughout her journey, Alice navigates shifting identities, paradoxical rules, and bizarre characters such as the Cheshire Cat, the Mad Hatter, and the Queen of Hearts, creating a timeless exploration of imagination and logic.
