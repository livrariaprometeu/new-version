Título original: Dracula

Autor: Bram Stoker



Published in 1897, Dracula is one of the most influential Gothic novels ever written and helped shape the modern image of vampires in popular culture. Told through letters, diary entries, newspaper articles, and telegrams, the story follows the mysterious Count Dracula as he leaves Transylvania for England, where he spreads fear and seeks new victims.

A group led by Professor Abraham Van Helsing joins forces to stop Dracula, facing themes of good versus evil, fear of the unknown, and the clash between modernity and ancient superstition.
